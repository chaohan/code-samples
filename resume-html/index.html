I have been working in the interconnected branch of photo-molecular physics and chemistry, where we utilize ultrafast laser light to design interaction scenarios of physical, chemical and applied-scientific interests. My doctoral research has covered topics spanning

  • coherent quantum control and quantum mechanics
  • linear, nonlinear and statistical optics, photonics and optical coherence
  • multiphoton and ultrafast optics
  • photonic structures and random lasers
  • incoherent optical and quantum dynamics
  • ultracold atoms and molecules.

My scientific knowledge also includes physical and quantum chemistry, computational physics and chemistry, spectroscopy, gas, solid state and many-body physics, and fundamental experimental instrumentation.
